Conway is the detection system that builds itself. It autonomously detects, investigates, and resolves suspicious alerts within your data.

From an analyst's desk, that means fewer rote investigations, automatically collected evidence, and every decision recorded for audit. Where you start in this guide depends on how familiar you already are with Conway:

- **New to Conway?** Start with [Core concepts](/core-concepts). The opening paragraph walks the full loop — run, investigation, verdict, case, disposition — in plain English, and the rest of the page defines each term precisely.
- **Already oriented?** Jump straight into [Triage investigations](/jobs/triage), the most common daily starting point.
- **Stuck on a screen?** Drop what you are looking at into the [chat assistant](/chat-overview) and ask.

<Note>
**On a Pilot deployment?** The **Response** and **Case work** sections describe the full product. Your Pilot has Detection, Investigation, and Knowledge only. See the [Pilot tab](/pilot/index) for what is connected and who to contact.
</Note>
